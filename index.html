How to Use the Whiteboard

  • Pen: Draw with a solid line. Change color and size using the controls.
  • Highlighter: Draw with a semi-transparent line, perfect for highlighting.
  • Eraser: Remove parts of your drawing by dragging over them.
  • Clear: Wipe the entire canvas clean and start over.
  • Save: Download your drawing as a PNG image file.
